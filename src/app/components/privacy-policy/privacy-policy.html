Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 22 July 2025

We are committed to protecting the privacy and security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, store, and disclose the data you provide when registering a trust with us.

1. Information We Collect

When you sign up to register a trust, we collect the following information:

  • Trust Information:
    • Trust Name
    • Trust Number
    • Establishment Date
    • Trust Email (optional)
    • List of Trustees
    • List of Beneficiaries (optional)
    • Documents Uploaded
    • Membership Status (e.g. Bullion Member)
    • Member Number (if applicable)
    • Referrer Number (if applicable)
  • Personal Information of the Primary Contact:
    • Full Name
    • ID Number
    • Email Address
    • Phone Number
  • Additional Metadata:
    • Payment Status (whether payment has been made)
    • Account Creation Date

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To register and manage your trust
  • To communicate with you regarding your trust and account
  • To process payments and confirm membership status
  • To meet compliance and legal obligations
  • To maintain internal records and security auditing
  • To personalize your user experience on our platform

3. Data Storage and Security

Your information is securely stored in encrypted databases and cloud storage systems with restricted access controls. We take reasonable steps to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information. We may share your information only in the following cases:

  • With authorized personnel managing your trust or processing documentation
  • With regulatory bodies, if required by law
  • With service providers who assist in hosting, document processing, or payment handling, under strict confidentiality agreements

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccuracies in your data
  • Request deletion of your personal data (subject to legal retention requirements)
  • Object to certain uses of your data
