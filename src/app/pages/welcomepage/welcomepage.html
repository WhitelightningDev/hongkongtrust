Trust Setup Service – Overview & Terms

A professional legal expert has developed the trust deed to provide the settlor with maximum control while minimizing potential tax implications for both the trust and its beneficiaries.

Key Details
  • You may act as both the settlor and one of the trustees.
  • Minimum two trustees must be appointed (including yourself).
  • Trustees cannot be beneficiaries. Beneficiaries may include family or another entity.
  • ID numbers or passport numbers are accepted.
  • A trust number will be issued even though it's not registered.
Structure & Control
  • This is an external service by AEM Co-op Ltd; a formal invoice will be issued.
  • No annual, registration, or accounting fees—you retain full control.
Important Note

AEM Co-op Ltd does not provide tax advice or legal opinions. You must ensure compliance with all applicable laws.

Features of a Hong Kong Foreign Trust
  • No registration required; no disclosure of settlor/beneficiary info.
  • No requirement for settlor, beneficiary, or trustee to be HK residents.
  • No licensing required for trustee.
  • Optional protector; 80-year max duration.
  • No asset/property restrictions; no specific asset protection law.
  • No reporting requirements; foreign income not taxable in HK.
