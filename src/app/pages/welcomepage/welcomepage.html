Application to Set Up Hong Kong Foreign Trust

Trust Setup Overview & Terms

A professional legal expert has developed the trust deed to provide the settlor with maximum control while minimizing potential tax implications for both the trust, the trustees and its beneficiaries.

Pricing
  • Standard Setup Fee: R7,500 / $420
  • Exclusive Offer for Bullion Members: R3,000 / $170
  • Payable upfront via EFT or cryptocurrency.
Key Details
  • You may act as both the settlor and one of the trustees.
  • Minimum two trustees must be appointed (including yourself).
  • For tax reasons, Trustees cannot be beneficiaries. Beneficiaries may include family or another entity.
  • ID numbers or passport numbers are accepted. Preferable passport numbers.
  • A trust number will be issued even though it's not registered anywhere.
Structure & Control
  • No annual fees, no registration fees or accounting fees—except what you as Trustee want to keep record of. You retain full control.
  • You can open a bank account or any other account anywhere in the world where it is accepted.
  • The trust can be a member of Bullion Limited.
Features of a Hong Kong Foreign Trust
  • No registration anywhere; no disclosure of settlor/beneficiary info.
  • No requirement for settlor, beneficiary, or trustee to be HK residents.
  • Protector appointment optional. 80-year max duration.
  • No asset/property restrictions; no specific asset protection law.
  • No reporting requirements; foreign income not taxable in HK.
Soft Tips You May Consider
  • Choose a name for the trust that has an Eastern flavour to it.
  • If you have a passport, rather use it than IDs.
  • You act as settlor and trustee 1.
  • Consider spouse, auditor or friend to act as co-trustees.
  • Three trustees is a good number – especially when it comes to voting.
  • Beneficiaries – children or spouse (if not Trustee), other trusts or entities.
Important Note

We do not provide tax advice or legal opinions. You must ensure compliance with all applicable laws and structure your financial position in co-operation with your auditor.
